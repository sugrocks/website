Why a new website?

So, I'm working on this new template. I've been asking some friends about the (original) website and how they feel when using it. There are some problems like:
  • the website feels old with the original 4chan-y design
  • there's not enough space between some elements
  • the navigation bar is kind of ignored and people doesn't know there's a download page or where the OP template is
  • the thread list on the home page is horrible on mobile (73.9% of our visits are from a computer, the rest are from touch devices and consoles)
  • there should be some links related to /sug/ or SU which aren't in the OP
  • and more...
This is not the final design. If you see something that should be changed, you can send me a feedback with your ideas, links, screenshots, etc.
I'll be posting and replying to your feedback here. If you want to say something, hit me up on Discord, Twitter, by email or use the feedback form here.
